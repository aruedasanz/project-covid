About us

We are a group of highly motivated policy students at the Harvard Kennedy School who believe in the power of evidence to analyze and recommend policy. Through this site, we share this enthusiasm and give you some facts to better understand what is happening and what should be done. We built this website using agile product management methodology learned in DPI-691M Programming and Data for Policymakers taught by Prof. Dhrumil Mehta.

Fernando Gimenez, MPA/ID
