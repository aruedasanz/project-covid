About us!

We are students at the Harvard Kennedy School and built this website for DPI-691M Programming and Data for Policymakers taught by Prof. Dhrumil Mehta.

This site was developed using the agile product management methodology learned in the course.

We are a group of highly motivated policy students who believe in the power of evidence to analyze and recommend policy. In this site, we want to share this enthusiasm and give you some facts to better understand what is happening and what should be done.

Fernando Gimenez, MPA/ID

Fernando is a 2nd year MPA/ID student at the Harvard Kennedy School of Government. Over the last summer, he worked as a Summer Intern for UN-Habitat on climate finance. Before working there, he worked at Crisil Global Research & Analytics as research analyst of Latam equities. He was awarded the Fulbright-Provincia de San Juan scholarship and the Fortabat Fellowship to pursue his studies at Harvard as well as BCRA's 2016 Raul Prebisch Prize in Economic Research. He holds MA in Finance from Universidad de San Andres and a Bsc in Economics from Universidad Catolica de Cuyo. Linkedin
